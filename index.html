Art that speaks what words can’t.

Hi, I’m Alex.

I’m a student, artist, author and mental health advocate. I create emotional, expressive paintings about mental health, healing, identity, and everything we don’t always say out loud.

My journey into art began during one of the hardest periods of my life. Creativity became more than an escape — it became a way to heal, express emotions, and connect with others who felt misunderstood or alone.

My work is raw, honest, and full of feeling.

I want people to look at my work and feel seen.
